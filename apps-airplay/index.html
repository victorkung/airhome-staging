It takes two to tango

AirPlay needs Wi-Fi to dance, but you already have that.

When Airhome and your favorite apps show up, it’s party time.

Airhome, Apps, and AirPlay all work together when your devices are all connected on the same Wi-Fi network.

All devices connected to the Wi-Fi can AirPlay.

Airplay is easy as 1-2-3

Virtually limitless content

Enjoy anything you can AirPlay. Your content is only limited by the Internet. And the internet isn't going anywhere.

Airhome works with Apple

There are no remotes, no keypads, or cables. Airhome products work directly from your iPhone®, iPad®, Apple TV® or Apple Watch®.

AirPlay over Wi-Fi

Each wireless Airhome AMP uses Apple's AirPlay technology built-in almost all Apple products. Just swipe up and press AirPlay.

Don’t worry, Android can play wireless music at an Airhome party too.

Find out more→

Plays from your favorite apps.

Airhome lets you play music right from the source. No “middleman” software. Open your favorite music app and pick a room. Voila — instant heaven.

Recommended apps

Airhome + Apple TV = ♥

It gets better. Airhome lets you set up one of your five rooms with Apple TV for high-quality room-shaking, 5.1 surround sound. Again, no additional software. AirPlay works with Apple TV too. Airhome is AirPlay's faithful companion.

AirPlay With Apple TV

Party mode with iTunes®

Want to play the same music in every room? No problem. You can play in party mode with iTunes on Mac or PC. There’s a little button in the AirPlay menu to change from ‘single’ to ‘multiple’ speakers. When you select multiple speaker, you can play the same music in every room.

You can also remotely control the party from your iPhone using the Apple Remote app.

Party Mode With iTunesRemote App

Airhome works with Apple Watch too

With Apple Watch you simply start playing music from your favorite music apps, press and hold the screen using Force Touch to reveal the AirPlay icon, then pick a room. Voila - instant heaven.

AirPlay With Apple Watch

For iPhone and iPad just swipe up

No matter what you watch or listen to on iPhone or iPad, all you need to do is swipe up to reveal the control center, press AirPlay, and select the room you’re in.

AirPlay With iPhone and iPad

Control freak? There’s an Airhome app for that.

With the free Airhome app, everyone has a remote. If the kids get carried away in the basement, Mom can Mute. If the game is too quiet in the Den, Dad can turn it up!

You play all your music, movies, and TV shows from your favorite apps, so the Airhome app is not required. But once you get rockin’ with your new Airhome system, there’s some really cool things you can do with it.

Control volume, mute, and mute all for every Airhome Amp
