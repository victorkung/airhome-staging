FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I play music on Airhome with an Anroid® device?

Yes. Airhome Amps are network audio devices that connect to your Wi-Fi® and become an addressable network point, otherwise referred to as an IP. There are various Android apps that can connect and play music to any available network audio device such as an Airhome Amp. Due to Android's open source platform, the method by which each app connects to Airhome is different, but there many existing 3rd party apps and always new ones under development that can work with Airhome.

Is there an Airhome app for Android?

No. There currently is not an Airhome app available for Android®, however, it's important to note that the Airhome app does not play music on Airhome. The Airhome app is simply used to configure new Airhome Amps, or if the Amps are already configured, the Airhome app can control product features such as volume, mute, and equalization.
