Bring the music home

Homeowners love what they see. And they crave what they can't see — an easy-to-use, high-quality wireless music system. That's Airhome.

Airhome Multiroom Audio System

A house full of music, minus the clutter.

It's all about the AMPs

Each AMP40 contains a Wi-Fi® chip and drives 40 watts of high efficiency class D digital audio amplification in stereo 2.1 at 8Ω

Each AMP5.1S also contains a Wi-Fi® chip and drives 75 watts of class D digital audio amplification in 5.1 surround sound at 8Ω

Plus, you can optionally connect a self-powered (active) subwoofer to every AMP for added bass response.

*AMP40 & AMP5.1S are sold separately.

But it all starts with the panel.

The Airhome panel is a wall mounted fixture. It’s patented design is an industry first and a revolution in home audio. First you wire the panel to in-ceiling speakers throughout the house. Next, add AMPs to activate each room of audio. Each AMP will power 1 room. That’s 5 rooms of wired audio for every complete system. Need more rooms? Just add more panels.

Airhome Audio Capabilities

Up to 5 rooms of audio

Play different music in up to 5 rooms or play party mode from iTunes®. All music is sourced directly from a laptop, Mac or PC, or any iOS device. No media servers required.

4 rooms of audio

2.1 just means 2 speakers + 1 subwoofer. Each Airhome panel can accommodate up to 4 rooms of stereo sound. You can play different music in every room, or the same music in every room from iTunes®.

1 room of surround sound

Setup one of your rooms with high-quality, room-shaking surround sound. 5.1 just means 5 speakers + 1 subwoofer. This room works great for watching movies and TV shows on Apple TV®

Optional Subwoofer in every room.

The Airhome provides an option to wire and connect a self-powered (active) subwoofer in every room. With a subwoofer you can really hear and feel the difference in every room.
